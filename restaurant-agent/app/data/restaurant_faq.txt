==============================
Restaurant FAQ & Policies
==============================

Q: What are your opening hours?
A: Most restaurants are open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

Q: Are opening hours different on holidays?
A: Yes. Some restaurants may have adjusted hours on public holidays.

Q: Can I reserve a table online?
A: Yes. Customers can reserve tables through our online reservation system.

Q: Can I reserve a table by phone?
A: Yes. Reservations can be made by phone or through the online system.

Q: Is there any reservation fee?
A: No. Table reservations are completely free.

Q: How far in advance can I book a table?
A: Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance.

Q: Can I reserve a table for today?
A: Yes, subject to table availability.

Q: How many people can a table accommodate?
A: Tables are available for 2, 4, 6, and 8 guests.

Q: Can I reserve multiple tables?
A: Yes. Multiple tables can be reserved for large groups.

Q: Can I book a table for a large group?
A: Yes. Group reservations are available depending on table availability.

Q: Is a deposit required for large group bookings?
A: Yes. Groups above a certain size may be asked to pay a deposit to confirm the reservation.

Q: Is a service charge added to the bill?
A: Some restaurants add a service charge for larger groups; this is usually mentioned at the time of booking.

Q: Can I book a private event?
A: Yes. Selected restaurants offer private dining and event bookings.

Q: Can I book a birthday party?
A: Yes. Birthday reservations are available.

Q: Can I book a business meeting?
A: Yes. Restaurants welcome corporate reservations.

Q: Can I modify my reservation?
A: Yes. Reservations can be modified before the scheduled time.

Q: Can I cancel my reservation?
A: Yes. Reservations can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the reservation time.

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: Refunds are issued for cancelled orders or reservations according to restaurant policy, usually within 5-7 business days.

Q: Is there a no-show fee?
A: Some restaurants may charge a no-show fee for large group reservations that are not cancelled in advance.

Q: What happens if I arrive late?
A: Your table will be held for 15 minutes after the reserved time.

Q: What happens if I do not arrive?
A: The reservation will be marked as a no-show and the table may be released.

Q: How do I know if a table is available?
A: The reservation system checks table availability in real time.

Q: Can I request a window seat?
A: Yes. Special seating requests are accepted but cannot be guaranteed.

Q: Can I choose a specific table?
A: Table preferences can be requested but depend on availability.

Q: Do restaurants provide takeaway?
A: Yes. Most restaurants offer takeaway services.

Q: Do restaurants provide home delivery?
A: Many restaurants offer home delivery depending on the location.

Q: How can I check if delivery is available?
A: Delivery availability depends on the restaurant and delivery area.

Q: Can I order food without a reservation?
A: Yes. Reservations are not required for food orders.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Cash, debit cards, credit cards, and digital wallets are accepted.

Q: Can I pay online?
A: Yes. Online payment is available for supported restaurants.

Q: Are taxes included in menu prices?
A: Taxes are calculated according to local regulations and may appear during checkout.

Q: Do menu prices change seasonally?
A: Yes. Some restaurants update menu prices and items seasonally based on ingredient availability.

Q: Do restaurants offer discounts?
A: Selected restaurants provide seasonal promotions and discounts.

Q: Is tipping expected?
A: Tipping is optional and at the customer's discretion unless a service charge is already included.

Q: Do restaurants have vegetarian dishes?
A: Yes. Vegetarian options are available at most restaurants.

Q: Do restaurants serve vegan food?
A: Many restaurants include vegan menu items.

Q: Are gluten-free meals available?
A: Some restaurants offer gluten-free dishes.

Q: Are halal meals available?
A: Availability depends on the restaurant's menu.

Q: How do you handle food allergies?
A: Customers can inform the restaurant of any allergies, and staff will advise on suitable menu items.

Q: Can I view the menu before visiting?
A: Yes. Menus are available online.

Q: Can I search food by category?
A: Yes. Menu items can be filtered by category.

Q: Can I search menu items by price?
A: Yes. Food items can be searched by price range.

Q: Can I see restaurant ratings?
A: Yes. Ratings are displayed for every restaurant.

Q: Can I leave a review?
A: Yes. Customers can submit ratings and reviews after their visit.

Q: Can I search restaurants by cuisine?
A: Yes. Restaurants can be searched by cuisine such as Indian, Chinese, Italian, Thai, American, and Mexican.

Q: Can I search restaurants by location?
A: Yes. Restaurants can be searched by city or location.

Q: Can I book restaurants in different cities?
A: Yes. Restaurants are available in multiple cities including Manchester, Leeds, Birmingham, Bristol, London, and Liverpool.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Parking availability depends on the restaurant.

Q: Do restaurants provide free Wi-Fi?
A: Many restaurants offer complimentary Wi-Fi.

Q: Is there a dress code?
A: Dress code policies vary by restaurant; casual attire is generally accepted unless stated otherwise.

Q: Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
A: Most restaurants offer wheelchair accessible entrances and seating; availability may vary by location.

Q: Are pets allowed?
A: Pet policies vary by restaurant.

Q: Are children welcome?
A: Yes. Restaurants are family friendly.

Q: Do restaurants have children's menus?
A: Many restaurants offer children's meals.

Q: Can I bring my own cake?
A: Some restaurants allow outside cakes for celebrations. Please contact the restaurant in advance.

Q: How can I contact a restaurant?
A: Restaurant contact information is available on the restaurant profile.

Q: Can I reorder a previous meal?
A: Yes. Previous orders can be viewed and reordered if available.

Q: How do I report an issue with my order?
A: Contact customer support through the application or restaurant contact details.

Q: Can I request special cooking instructions?
A: Yes. Special requests can be added during ordering, subject to restaurant approval.

Q: Does the system show restaurants with table booking?
A: Yes. Restaurants supporting table reservations are displayed accordingly.
